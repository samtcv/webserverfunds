BEERLYMPICS

Welcome to the Beerlympics! A annual event lasting a full weekend where we play games some drinking games some not to gain points where the prize is the Beerlympics trophee!

Some challenges and games include:

  1. 24 beers in 24h
  2. Kayak Relay
  3. Lazer Tag
  4. Washer Toss
  5. Beer Pong
  6. Cop Stop
  7. Hot Potato
  8. Water Ballon Toss
  9. Knockout
